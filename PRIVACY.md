# Privacy Policy — BTL Dashboard (ביטוח לאומי — לוח מצב)

_Last updated: 28 June 2026_

## Summary
BTL Dashboard is a **read-only, fully local** browser extension. It does **not**
collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything runs inside your
own browser.

## What the extension accesses
After **you** log in to the National Insurance personal area
(`https://ps.btl.gov.il`), the extension reuses your existing browser session to
read the same information the portal already shows you — insurance/health-payment
status, debts, reserve-duty (miluim) entitlements, letters, and past inquiries.
These are read-only inquiry requests to `ps.btl.gov.il` only.

## What the extension does NOT do
- It does **not** ask for, see, or store your password.
- It does **not** send any data to the developer or to any third party.
- It does **not** use analytics, tracking, or remote code.
- It performs **no** payments, edits, claim submissions, or uploads.

## Data storage
Fetched data is held **in memory only** (`chrome.storage.session`) to render the
dashboard, and is automatically discarded when you close the browser. No data is
written to any server.

## Permissions
- **`storage`** — to hold the fetched dashboard data in session memory only.
- **Host access to `https://ps.btl.gov.il/*`** — to read your own data from the
  National Insurance portal while you are logged in. No other site is accessed.
